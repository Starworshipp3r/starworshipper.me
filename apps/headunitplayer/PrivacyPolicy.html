Headunit Player Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Overview

Headunit Player is a local music player for Android devices and car headunits. It is designed to scan and play audio files stored on your device.

The app does not use accounts, advertising, analytics, cloud sync, or internet access.

Data Accessed

Headunit Player asks for audio or storage permission so it can find and play local music files on your device.

The app may read local audio metadata such as track title, artist, album, duration, codec information, embedded artwork, waveform data, and loudness information. This information is used only to display and play your local music library inside the app.

Data Collection and Sharing

Headunit Player does not transmit your music files, listening history, metadata, artwork, settings, or any other app data to the developer or to third parties.

The app does not contain third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

On-Device Storage

Headunit Player stores settings, playback position, shuffle queue state, waveform cache, artwork cache, and loudness cache on your device. These files are used to make playback and library browsing faster.

This app data remains on your device unless you clear the app data, uninstall the app, or remove the relevant music files from your device.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because Headunit Player does not collect data on a server, there is no remote account or server-side data to delete.

You can delete app settings and cached data at any time through Android Settings by clearing Headunit Player's app data. You can also uninstall the app to remove its local app data.

Permissions

On newer Android versions, Headunit Player uses audio access permission to scan local songs. On older Android versions, Android may present storage access permission for the same purpose.

Headunit Player does not request internet permission.

Children

Headunit Player is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when Headunit Player changes. The effective date above will be updated when the policy changes.
